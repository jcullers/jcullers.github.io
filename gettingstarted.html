Tips and Other Advice

You can fish in a lot of places in Idaho, I have many lakes and reservoirs I like to fish on but here are my top 3

  1. CJ Strike
  2. Cascade
  3. Dworshak reservoir

>Additionally, I enjoy catching several types of fish.
Here are some of my favorites in no particular order

  • Largemouth Bass
  • Bluegill
  • Rainbow trout

I also produce a weekly fishing podcast

It is called the Southern Idaho Fishing Report

The Southern Idaho fishing report is a weekly podcast that lets anglers know what the conditions are going to be like for the upcoming week.
This report allows anglers to better prepare where to fish or if they should stay home.



This links to my fishing podcast website

While the Southern Idaho Fishing Report podcast is published weekly, listeners can go back in to hear past shows.

Archived Show Access

In order to access past shows, go to the show library and click on archived shows and here you will find shows from the past. While the weather, stocking, and water levels will no longer be accurate; the hot tips and interviews will be of interest to listeners.
